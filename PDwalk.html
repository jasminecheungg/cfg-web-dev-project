Walk the Peak District - Mam Tor

Why you should visit?

The stone surfaced footpath from the car park to the top of Mam
Tor will reward you with one of the most dramatic vistas in the Peak District.
From the viewpoint you can see north over the Edale Valley to Kinder Scout and
the Derwent Moors. This circular route takes you round Mam Tor and has some amazing scenery.
